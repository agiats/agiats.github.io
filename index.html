About me

Taiga Someya is a Master’s student at the Department of Language and Information Sciences, Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, University of Tokyo. He is a member of Oseki Lab. His research interests include Natural Language Processing/Computational Linguistics, Computer Vision and Soccer.
